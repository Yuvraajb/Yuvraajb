## About Me

I'm a high school student and indie iOS developer based in San Jose, CA. I build fully **on-device AI-powered apps** using Apple's FoundationModels framework — no backends, no cloud, just intelligence running right on the device. My work sits at the intersection of thoughtful UX, cinematic design, and real AI capability.

Currently shipping apps to the App Store and exploring what's possible when Apple Intelligence meets great product design.

### Mission Artemis
**Interactive Lunar Mission Simulator**

An immersive, educational iOS app that lets you experience NASA's Artemis II crewed lunar flyby mission firsthand. Features a cinematic onboarding sequence, real orbital mechanics, crew AI chat, and engineering challenges at each mission phase.
